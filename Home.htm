Electronic waste, also known as 'E-waste', is where the developing countries profit off burning computer components to extract out iron, gold, and copper to sell them for money. The problem is that the process to extract the metals are causing immense amount of pollution and is harming the employees from the toxins that are released from the burning. In Guiyu, the drinking water is needed to be trucked, while the citizens have serious health issues.
